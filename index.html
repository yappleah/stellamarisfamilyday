About Us

Presented by the Stella Maris Church Fundraising Committee, this event celebrates the joy of the Christmas season while supporting vital church programs and outreach.

It's all about faith, family, food, and fun with Jamaican flair! Join us for an unforgettable celebration that brings our community together in the spirit of Christmas.

Experience authentic Jamaican culture, delicious traditional food, live entertainment, and activities for the whole family in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
